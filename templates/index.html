How the engine works

Every track is reduced to a feature vector — energy, danceability, tempo, and popularity — normalized to a comparable scale.

Recommendations use weighted Euclidean distance: energy (30%) + danceability (30%) + tempo (20%) + popularity (20%). Closer in feature space = higher match confidence.

Mood classification applies threshold logic across energy and danceability to sort tracks into ten distinct sonic categories.
