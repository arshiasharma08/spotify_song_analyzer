How It Works

This platform uses weighted Euclidean distance to find similar songs. Each track is analyzed across normalized audio features: energy, danceability, tempo, and popularity.

The recommendation engine calculates similarity with weighted importance: Energy (30%) + Danceability (30%) + Tempo (20%) + Popularity (20%).

Click mood cards to filter songs by vibe, search for specific tracks, or use Song Battle to compare any two tracks.
