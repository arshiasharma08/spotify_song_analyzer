How This Works

Recommendation Algorithm

This platform uses weighted Euclidean distance to measure similarity between songs. Each song is analyzed across four normalized audio features to find your next favorite track.

  • Energy (0-1): Intensity and activity level
  • Danceability (0-1): Suitability for dancing
  • Tempo (BPM): Speed in beats per minute
  • Popularity (0-100): Relative popularity score

Why Normalization Matters

All metrics are normalized to a 0-1 scale for fair comparison. Without normalization, a 20 BPM difference would overshadow a 0.2 energy difference, even though they're equally important.
